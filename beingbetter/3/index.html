The 11 secrets for online success

1. Consistency

Create Content Regularly

  • Regularly doesn't mean daily
  • It can be once in two days, once in a week or even once in a month
  • Consistent content creates more impressions, and helps build brand

My Stats (Avg)

  • 2 times a day on LinkedIn
  • Once a week on YouTube
  • Biweekly YouTube live (The Lean Talks)
  • Weekly Medium Blogs
  • And other (unevenly) distributed content on Twitter/Instagram

2. Sincerity

Put authentic data

Click-bait posts will degrade your overall quality

3. Give back, GENUINELY!

It might be very difficult in a busy schedule, but still, when you give your time, you are actually able to connect better

Try to mentor, and guide genuinely (We rise by lifting others)

Some of my initiatives in 2020

  • Web Mentor Program
  • DailyJS
  • The Lean Talks)
  • Web Mentor Webinars
  • Being Better

4. Share helpful resources

People love the content where they get helpful resources

Simple, if any resource helped you in your journey, why not share it with others?

5. Make helpful content

Don't limit yourself to external helpful resources

From your experiences, create content that might help others

6. Stay relevant

Stay to the point in your posts

Don't deviate, don't click-bait

7. Analyze

Metrics might differ from person to person, but try to measure your weekly/monthly growth

Numbers might vary everytime, and you shouldn't feel bad for posts that don't perform well

But, analytics can help you understand how can you create better content

8. Storytell

Human minds are bad at remembering facts, but good at remembering stories

9. Be open minded

Learn from constructive criticism and GROW!

Going to publish a video on

How to use feedback to your advantage and how to deal with criticism

Stay tuned to "Being Better" series

10. Be Humble

Humility and Empathy is the key

11. Keep viewers in Mind

It's about them, share what they will find useful/helpful
